FAQ



Are the matches knockout or league?
Depending on the number of participants different sports will have different formats. More the participants the more the likelihood of it being a knockout.

Accomodation? On/Off campus?
All female participants will be provided accomodation in campus. We will try to do the same for all male participants. If the number exceeds our capacity accomodation will be provided elsewhere close to the campus.

Is it necessary that the team have to participate for all the sports from a particular college?
No, you will have to register before the event to be able to take part but you can take part in fun events.

Is there any proof we need to bring when we come to pay?
You are required to get the latest fee receipt of college and an original ID proof issued by the institute for each participant.

If I am not able to attend any sport, will l get a refund?
Registration fees are supposed to be paid within a week of your registration. Once paid, there will be no refund issued in any case.
